### Personal Reflection (2026)

Looking back at this post, there's one thing I didn't mention in the official write-up.

On Day 2 of the conference, I had to give the opening remarks. And I decided, for some reason, that it would be a good idea to **sing**.

The song was *Celestial* by Ed Sheeran. Our theme that year was **Gabay** (Guide), and the lyrics fit perfectly:

> *"You make me feel like my troubled heart is a million miles away"*

That line resonated with me. The PythonPH community was exactly that for me — a place where the stress and trouble of daily life just... faded. So I stood up in front of everyone and sang it.

Was it good? Debatable. Was it embarrassing? Absolutely. But the room was kind, and honestly, it captured how I felt about the community better than any formal speech could have. Sometimes the cringey moments become the most memorable ones.

That's what PyCon PH was about to me. Not just the talks and the workshops, but the fact that you could stand in front of 200+ people, sing an Ed Sheeran song, and they'd cheer for you anyway.

That's the Gabay spirit.
