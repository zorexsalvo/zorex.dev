I can finally say it: I'm officially a marathoner.

February 16, 2025. 42.195 kilometers. Months of early morning runs, long slow distances, and more chafing than I'd care to admit.

Huge shoutout to [@rwprunclub](https://instagram.com/rwprunclub) and [@rwp_rizal](https://instagram.com/rwp_rizal) for the support, motivation, and miles shared along the way. Proud to be a TBR Alumni Class of 2025!

It's not every day you cross a finish line after months of training. Grateful for everyone who ran alongside me, literally and figuratively.

Now if you'll excuse me, I need to figure out how stairs work again.
