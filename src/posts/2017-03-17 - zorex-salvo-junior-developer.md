I'm from the Philippines.

I've been coding professionally for a year now. I work as a developer at a startup, using Python and Django for most of my projects.

Being exposed to startup culture, I became fascinated with the whole tech community. I've attended hackathons, meetups, and summits ever since.

Being a developer at a startup is both exciting and challenging. We get to try a lot of modern and trending technologies — what we called our *new toys*. But in exchange, we have a lot to study. We have to be full-stack.

> *Eight years later: still learning, still building. Some things don't change.*
